    • New HomeGrid Forum G.hn Certification over New 2Gbps Coax Ethernet Bridge and Dual Band Wi-Fi Bridge Pass

      Issued : Friday, September 29, 2020

      Hsinchu, TAIWAN – – Covid-19 dramatically changes the daily life people used to live with since spring in 2020. Though we can’t surely see the dawn to come, yet we can surely realize this outspreading global wide disease has been entirely triggering our motivation to keep everything moving non-stop as possible as we can for learning, working, entertaining and caring. Undoubtedly, all of these demand are massively rely on remote access by reliable broadband networking devices.


      SendTek Corporation announces today the newest 2Gbps Coax Ethernet Bridge CES-833 and 2.4GHz/5GHz Dual Band Wi-Fi 2Gbps Coax Ethernet Bridge CES-862 both pass the G.hn Certification by HomeGrid Forum. With the reliable G.hn certified CES-833 and CES-862, people can access securely to the network by using their mobiles and portables for either working from home or learning at home, to minimize the effect from the lockdown by pandemic.



      In addition to home networking and LAN extension, CES-833 and CES-862 can also support to work with Coax MDU Master Bridge CEM-839 or CEM-8392 for MDU access.
