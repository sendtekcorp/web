Introduction

THE G.HN COAX ETHERNET BRIDGE, 2GBPS CES-833 AND 1GBPS CES-832/831/830, ENABLES YOU TO SET UP YOUR PRIVATE NETWORK AT HOME WITH YOUR EXISTING COAXIAL CABLE.

NO ADDITIONAL NETWORK WIRING IS NECESSARY. YOU MAY JUST FIND A COAX CONNECTOR IN THE WALL, PUT THE COAXIAL PLUG INTO THE CONNECTOR, AND YOU CAN HAVE 2GBPS/1GBPS HOME NETWORK READY FOR THE BROADBAND INTERNET AND IPTV TRANSMISSION AMONG ROOMS.

INTELLIGENCE TO SUPPORT VARIANT SERVICES. CONFIGURATION OF TAG VLAN, QUEUING SCHEME, AND BANDWIDTH CONTROL HELP THE DEPLOYMENT OF SERVICES.

AS WELL AS FULL BAND 200MHZ CES-833, 100MHZ CES-832, SPECTRUM 76MHZ OPTION (CES-831) AND 48MHZ OPTION (CES-830) TO CO-EXIST WITH EXISTING CATV SIGNAL.

WITH CES-833/832/831/830 SERIES, SOHO CAN EVEN QUICKLY EXTEND OR FLEXIBLY CHANGE THE EXISTING LAN ENVIRONMENT. NO RE-WIRING AND NO MORE TROUBLE.

Features

  • G.hn Coax Port as Home Network Backbone by Using Existing Coaxial Cable
  • G.hn PHY Rate up to 2Gbps for CES-833, 1Gbps for CES-832/831/830
  • Two 10/100/1000Mbps Gigabit Ethernet Ports
  • Energy Efficiency (Green) Ethernet Support
